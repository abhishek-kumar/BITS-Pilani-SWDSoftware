Description:
  This project contains the codebase for 3 separate applications that were deployed
  at BITS Pilani - Goa Campus during 2005 to 2009 for 
    (1) Collecting and maintaining personal accident and health insurance information of students
    (2) Allocating students to hostels during initial registration
    (3) Collecting and maintaining information for scholarship applications of students

Structure:
  Each sub-project is within a separate directory: chas (hostel allotment), 
  Insurance (health insurance system), Scholarship (scholarship details IS)
